About Me

I am a Remote Sensing M.Sc. graduate from Shahid Beheshti University, passionate about integrating artificial intelligence with remote sensing to address critical challenges in natural hazard monitoring, sustainable agriculture, and environmental conservation. My master’s thesis focused on winter wheat yield estimation using Sentinel-2 imagery and deep learning (LSTM) models, achieving robust predictive accuracy. With a strong foundation in geospatial analysis and machine learning, I aim to pursue a PhD to develop innovative AI-driven remote sensing solutions for disaster risk management, flood and drought monitoring, and precision agriculture.
